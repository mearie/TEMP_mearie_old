[mearie.org](mearie) is a personal website of [Kang Seonghoon](kang-seonghoon). Kang Seonghoon is a South Korean software developer engaged in several free and open source software projects. mearie.org is one of dozen websites hosted on [ruree.net](http://ruree.net/) server, and `mearie.org` domain is strictly dedicated to my creations and/or related thingies.

*Mearie*, spelled <span lang="ko">메아리</span> in Hangeul, means an echo in Korean. (You may note the romanization is not standard. This is intended.)
