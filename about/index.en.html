About mearie.org

mearie.org is a personal website of Kang Seonghoon. This domain is operated since 2007, and solely represents my creations and opinions. "Mearie" is a transliteration of Korean word 메아리You may note that this website's logo is an initial from that word., meaning echo.

mearie.org is XHTML 1.0 Strict and CSS 2 valid, and is viewable with any browser. mearie.org is hosted by ruree.net, and made with Python , Mako templating library and mearieflo.
